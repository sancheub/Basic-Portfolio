About me:


I’m a person of broken habits. A curious one. Always looking for the learning of something new. New experiences, knowledge, new friends.

Victim of my own habits one day I realized my actions had been the same across my life. But something like that is not easy for the self esteem and I had to made a choice: To change every day something, even if it was a small detail. Running by a different path, tasting a new food or watching an interesting documentary… by doing these different things day by day the habits and behaviors change preparing our person to future adventures.
Then, walking in this way I discovered beautiful panoramas hiking early in a still dark morning, amazing movies, unbelievable stories hidden in five thousand-pages books, traveling to places I never had thought to visit, living experiences and achieving tons of good friends I wouldn’t have had if I was hidden in the room of my old customs.

It’s a good habit building new habits.
